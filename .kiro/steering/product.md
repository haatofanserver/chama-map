# Product Overview

**Haachama Radar** is an interactive web application that tracks and displays the locations visited by Akai Haato (Haachama), a VTuber from Hololive, across Japan's 47 prefectures.

## Core Features

- Interactive map of Japan showing prefecture boundaries
- Visual highlighting of visited prefectures
- Track markers showing specific locations with photos, tweets, and YouTube streams
- Multi-language support (English/Japanese)
- Trip details and media links for each location
- Progress visualization of prefecture coverage
- PWA support with offline capabilities

## Target Audience

- Akai Haato/Haachama fans
- VTuber community members interested in "Seichi-junrei" (pilgrimage to locations)
- Users interested in Japan travel and geography

## Key Goals

- Help fans discover and visit locations Haachama has been to
- Provide a respectful, informative travel companion
- Support fan community engagement through shared experiences
- Maintain accuracy and cultural sensitivity

## Content Management

- Manual content entry (no auto-scraping)
- Google My Maps as backend data source
- Fan-submitted content integration capability
- Curated media links (tweets, photos, YouTube streams)
